# Short Video Scripts — English

**Iran Has a Future** — Iran has a future. The Iran Prosperity Project is the plan to reach it. When the Islamic Republic collapses, chaos will not follow. Instead, a free Iran will rise on a foundation built years in advance for secular democracy and economic revival. Reza Pahlavi, who created this plan, will guide the nation to a constitutional referendum. Whether you're in Iran or far from home, this future belongs to you. Follow us, and share the plan with the world.

**What Is a Transitional Leader?** — What is a transitional leader? Rebuilding a nation takes stability, time to write a constitution and safely hold elections. That demands someone trusted not just to hold power, but to hand it back to the people. Across Iran, crowds chant the name of Reza Pahlavi, son of the exiled King. His supporters point to decades of advocacy and a vision for democracy. Pahlavi created the Iran Prosperity Project, a roadmap for transition. The plan runs deep. Follow along as we cover it all.

**Returning to Democracy** — Iran is ready to return to democracy. Forty thousand Iranians just gave their lives to prove that. A century ago, Iranians built a constitutional democracy, today they are risking everything to win it back. Whether the world stands with them or stays silent, they will not stop until their country is free. The Iran Prosperity Project is the plan that turns that sacrifice into a bright future — a roadmap to a government that works for its people. Follow along for the details and we will see you in a free Iran.

**A Captive Nation** — Does Iran's government speak for its people? No it does not. In a 2023 survey, over 80% of Iranians rejected Islamic rule of any kind. They have taken to the streets again and again, and each time the regime answered with arrests, executions, and mass murder. A government that must kill its own people to survive is not a government. It is an occupation. The Iranian people want something else: a democratic future. How will they reach it? Follow to find out.
